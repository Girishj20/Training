About MedPredict AI

An advanced clinical decision support system designed to assist healthcare professionals in predicting patient treatment costs and evaluating surgical requirements.

Predictive Intelligence

The platform utilizes optimized Random Forest estimators trained on historical patient records. The models analyze demographics, clinical vitals (blood pressure, oxygen levels, sugar), and admission stay details to generate cost and surgical estimates.

  • Regressor R² Score: 73.16%
  • Classifier Accuracy: 99.80%

Clinical Safeguards

Designed as a decision support tool, MedPredict AI includes built-in polypharmacy warnings, pre-operative fasting alerts, and disease-specific dietary guidelines. All outputs are intended to complement, not replace, professional clinical judgment.
